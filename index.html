KV MZU

This is KV MZU

KENDRIYA VIDYALAYA, MZU CAMPUS was established in 2010 and it is managed by the Central Govt.. It is located in Urban area. It is located in BUNGKAWN block of AIZAWL district of Mizoram. The school consists of Grades from 1 to 8. The school is Co-educational and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section. The school is Not Applicable in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. English is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April.
